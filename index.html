Crazy Driver VideoGame

This is my first video game created with libgdx.

** How yo play Crazy Driver ** Use the _arrow keys _to move around the game. This game has two levels, the first level you must collect five people avoiding cars. Roses enemies slow down your car. At the second level the cars go faster so that the difficulty also increases must collect ten people.

It's easy! Enjoy it!
